Emergency Kit Checklist

  • Sanitizer or Soap
  • Drinking Water (At least 1 litre/person/day)
  • Dry Snacks (e.g., biscuits, rusk, etc.)
  • Basic First Aid Kit along with daily medicines
  • Important Documents
  • Battery Operated Pocket Radio
  • Hygiene Kit (sanitary pads, toothbrush, paste)
  • Solar or Battery Operated Torch and Candle with Matchbox
  • Assistive Devices (used by persons with disabilities or elderly)
  • Whistle (to attract first responders if needed)
  • One Pair of Dress
  • Minimum Cash & ATM Card
  • Mobile, Charger & Power Bank
  • Mask (cloth preferred for reuse)
